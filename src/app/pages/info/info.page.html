OWT - keeping track of your weight Open Weight Tracker A leightweight (no pun intended) weight-tracker, focusing on simplicity and privacy! OWT does not require any registration, nor does it save your tracked data on any remote server. All your data stays on your own device. No advertisments, no hidden terms of use, no lock-in, everything open source! Keep track of your weight, completely anonymous and without a hassle. 1) Wake up, make coffee, morning-routine 2) Go to the toilet, pee and crap like there is no tomorrow 3) Stand on your scales and weigh your weight. 4) Open up OWT on your favorite device (e.g. smart-phone) and track your weight
